## 7. Delete My Data

You may request deletion of your data by:

- Using the in-app "Delete My Data" option (if available), or
- Contacting us directly

Deleting data removes:

- Game progress
- Stored runs and settings

Some anonymized or aggregated data may remain for analytics purposes.

- Know what personal information is collected

You may exercise your rights by:

- Using the in-app "Delete My Data" feature, or

We will respond to verified requests within the time required by applicable law.

Your data may be processed outside your country of residence.
